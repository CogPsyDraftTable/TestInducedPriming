Memory Task

 

Time: This task takes approximately 10-15 minutes. You will be compensated $0.50 for completing the hit.

 

Task:  You will be shown a list of words and tested on your memory for those words.

 

In the first phase, you will see a list of words presented very quickly. It is important to pay close attention to the words, because they will be shown very briefly.

 

In the second phase, you will be tested on your memory for the words you saw. You will see one word at a time and judge whether the word was OLD (shown in the first phase), or NEW (not shown in the first phase). You also be asked to judge whether you are confident or guessing for each trial.
